Read this before you pay

Requirements you must meet

This is a hands-on production cohort. You must supply your own tools. AIMG does not provide laptops, software, licenses, subscriptions, or internet access, and does not have loaner machines. If you cannot meet these requirements, please do not enroll — come to a free Film Bar AI Tuesday instead and talk to us first.

1 · Your own computer, powerful enough to do the work

You will run professional creative software: DaVinci Resolve, Blender, the Adobe Creative Suite, Higgsfield, and similar tools. These are demanding programs. A machine that only browses the web will not work, and you will not be able to complete the coursework on one.

Meets the requirement

  • Mac — Apple silicon (M1 or newer) with 16 GB unified memory or more. An 8 GB M1 will struggle badly in Blender and Resolve.
  • Windows — Intel Core i7 (10th gen or newer) or AMD Ryzen 7, 16 GB RAM minimum (32 GB recommended), and a dedicated GPU with at least 6 GB of VRAM — an NVIDIA RTX 3060 or better.
  • Both — a 512 GB SSD with at least 100 GB free, a current OS (macOS 14+ / Windows 11), and a reliable internet connection. Several of our tools are cloud-based.

Does not meet the requirement

  • Chromebooks
  • iPads, tablets, and phones
  • Laptops with integrated graphics only, and no dedicated GPU (most budget and ultra-thin Windows laptops)
  • Any machine with 8 GB of RAM or less

Unsure whether your laptop qualifies? Bring it to a Film Bar AI Tuesday and we'll look at it with you, for free, before you spend anything.

2 · Your own software and subscriptions

DaVinci Resolve and Blender are free. Adobe Creative Cloud, Higgsfield, and any other paid tool are your responsibility, at your own cost. Tuition does not include software, licenses, subscriptions, cloud credits, or hardware.

3 · Attendance, and what happens if you miss a Saturday

You are required to attend all eight Saturday sessions. If you miss one, a recording of that session will be made available to you, and you remain responsible for completing and submitting that week's assignment on time. Recordings are a backstop, not a substitute — the critique happens in the room, and it is the part that works.

Missing sessions does not entitle you to a refund, a make-up session, an extension, or a transfer to a later cohort.

4 · Tuition and refunds

Tuition is $800, paid in full at the time you enroll. We do not accept deposits, partial payments, or installments, and paying is what reserves your seat — there is no acceptance step.

Because seats are capped at twenty and your payment takes one out of the room, tuition is non-refundable once the cohort begins on July 18, 2026. Before that date, contact us and we will consider a refund at our discretion.

AIMG is not responsible for your inability to complete the coursework as a result of inadequate, incompatible, or malfunctioning equipment, missing software, or loss of internet access. The cohort prepares you for AI industry careers; it does not guarantee a job, a placement, an interview, or any particular income.
